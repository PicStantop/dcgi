A Place Where Chains Are Broken

Dominion Christian Gathering International is more than a church — it is a spiritual home where lives are transformed by the power of God's Word. Founded by Apostle Chika Jude Monye, our ministry has grown from a passionate vision into a vibrant, multi-branch community of faith.

Our name defines our calling: Dominion — the God-given authority to rule and reign in life. Christian — rooted in Christ Jesus. Gathering — because community is at the heart of our mission. International — because our reach and vision know no borders.

What We Stand For

Our Ministry Pillars

Powerful Worship

We believe in the transforming power of sincere, Spirit-led worship that draws believers into the very presence of the Almighty God.

Sound Teaching

Every sermon is rooted firmly in the uncompromising Word of God, equipping believers to live victoriously in every area of life.

Community & Family

We are a family — a community of believers who love, support, and grow together, carrying each other's burdens in true fellowship.

Global Vision

DCGI carries an international mandate to reach the nations with the liberating gospel of Jesus Christ, from Delta State to the world.

Healing & Miracles

We serve a God who still heals, delivers, and performs miracles. Through faith and prayer, we witness God's supernatural power regularly.

Compassion & Outreach

Driven by the love of Christ, we extend practical compassion to the poor, the broken, and the marginalised in our communities.
