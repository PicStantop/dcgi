Welcome Home

A Place Where Chains Are Broken

Dominion Christian Gathering International is more than a church — it is a spiritual home where lives are transformed by the power of God's Word. Founded by Apostle Chika Jude Monye, our ministry has grown from a passionate vision into a vibrant, multi-branch community of faith.

Our name defines our calling: Dominion — the God-given authority to rule and reign in life. Christian — rooted in Christ Jesus. Gathering — because community is at the heart. International — because our reach knows no borders.

What We Stand For

Our Ministry Pillars

Powerful Worship

We believe in Spirit-led worship that draws believers into the very presence of the Almighty God.

Sound Teaching

Every sermon is rooted firmly in the uncompromising Word of God, equipping believers to live victoriously.

Community & Family

We are a family who love, support, and grow together, carrying each other's burdens in true fellowship.

Global Vision

DCGI carries an international mandate to reach the nations with the liberating Gospel of Christ.

Healing & Miracles

We serve a God who still heals and delivers. Through faith and prayer, we witness His power regularly.

Compassion & Outreach

Driven by Christ's love, we extend practical compassion to the poor and marginalised in our communities.
