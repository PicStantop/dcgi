Our Story

Who We Are

Dominion Christian Gathering International (DCGI) is a Bible-believing, Spirit-filled Christian ministry founded on the unwavering truth of God's Word. Incorporated on 27th May, 2016 under the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (CAC/IT/NO 87720), DCGI has grown from a passionate vision into a vibrant, multi-branch community of believers.

Our founding mandate was clear: to raise a generation of believers who walk in the full authority and dominion that Christ purchased for them at Calvary. We believe every believer is called to live victoriously — free from every form of bondage, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and walking in the purpose of God.

Under the visionary leadership of Apostle Chika Jude Monye, DCGI has expanded across Nigeria with locations in Delta State, Edo State, and Lagos State, touching thousands of lives with the transforming power of the Gospel.

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me." — Philippians 4:13
Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

01

Our Mission

To preach the undiluted Gospel of Jesus Christ, raising disciples who walk in spiritual dominion, experience divine healing, and demonstrate the Kingdom of God in every sphere of life — family, society, and nation.

02

Our Vision

To see an army of Spirit-filled believers thoroughly equipped with God's Word, actively transforming their communities and reaching the nations with the liberating power of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Biblical Authority

Every teaching and decision is anchored entirely in the Holy Scriptures — God's infallible, eternal Word.

Prayer & Intercession

We are a house of prayer. Intercession is the engine of our ministry and the foundation of every breakthrough.

Discipleship

Making disciples is our calling. We invest in people's spiritual growth through mentoring and community.

Divine Healing

We believe in a God who still heals today. Signs, wonders and miracles confirm the preaching of the Word.

Evangelism

The Great Commission is our commission. We carry the Gospel to streets, cities, and nations with passion.

Love & Integrity

We serve with love, act with integrity, and treat every person with the dignity Christ imparts to His children.
